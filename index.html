Box Pricing Calculator

Calculate precise costs for corrugated boxes with our Box Cost Pricing feature. Tailored for manufacturers, it considers material, size, and production factors to deliver accurate, customer-specific pricing in seconds.

  • Support cost calculation for Universal and Die Cut types of box. Ability to provide size in inch/cm/mm.
  • Automatically suggest the UPS (Units per Sheet) based on machine specification.
  • Displays Weight and Cost of each paper.
  • Ability to add different types of cost factors like Conversion, Wastage Percentage, Lamination, Punching, Transportation, Printing and Profit Percentage.

Product and Customer Management

Simple and intuitive UI to manage GSM, BF, Flute Factor, Machine Constant, Products and Customers related data.

  • Update price of multiple products with a single click.
  • One click export of products in excel.
  • Ability to search products based on Name, Customer and Box Type.
  • Ability to add custom notes that will be printed on Job Card every time.

Generates detailed Job Card in seconds.

  • Ability to generate a Job Card for a customer's product with details like P.O Number, Quantity, Order and Delivery Date.
  • Automatically calculates the number of Paper and Liner required based on Order quantity and UPS (Unit Per Sheet).
  • Detail Job Card printing with all the details like Flute, Lamination, Printing, Joint Type, Box in Bundle, UPS and custom notes.
  • Contains weight of 10 sheet of Paper and 10 liners for easy reference.
